Covid-19

stay safe, stay home

Staying safe and staying home are key recommendations for preventing the spread of COVID-19. By limiting contact with others, wearing masks, and practicing good hygiene, we can help to slow the spread of the virus and protect ourselves and others.

protect now

take steps to protect yourself

Wear A Face Mask

The mask acts as a barrier to prevent droplets from reaching others and can help to protect both the wearer and others from inhaling the virus.

learn more

Wash Your Hands

It helps to remove the virus and other germs from your skin, reducing the risk of infection.

Avoid Close Contact

Avoiding close contact is an important measure to prevent the spread of COVID-19.

What Are The Main Symptoms?

It's important to note that not all people with COVID-19 have all of these symptoms, and some people may have only mild symptoms or no symptoms at all. If you are experiencing symptoms, it's important to self-isolate and seek medical advice.

  • fever
  • Tiredness
  • Dry Cough
  • Sore Throat
  • Aches and Pains
  • Shortness of Breath
